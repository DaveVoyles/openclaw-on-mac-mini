💻 Web Chat

Use OpenClaw from your browser — no Slack needed. If you've used Gemini or ChatGPT, you'll feel right at home.

🤔 How is this like Gemini?

Web Chat works almost exactly like Google Gemini — you type a question, the AI answers. The biggest differences are who can see it and where it runs.

🔵 Google Gemini

  • Type a question, get an answer
  • Keeps your chat history
  • Works in your browser
  • Free to use
  • Google can see your chats
  • Answers trained on the internet

🟣 OpenClaw Web Chat

  • Type a question, get an answer
  • Keeps your chat history
  • Works in your browser
  • Free to use
  • Private — only your family
  • Can use multiple AI models
Bottom line: If you already know how to use Gemini, you already know how to use Web Chat. Same idea — just private, just for you.

👤 Do I need my own account?

Yes — and it only takes a minute. Each person has their own private account. Dave's account is separate from yours, and your chats are completely your own.

⭐ Your account is independent

Chuck and Lisa each get their own login. You won't see each other's conversations, and Dave can't see them either (unless you choose to share).

Think of it like having your own Gmail inbox — Dave set up the service, but your messages are private to you.

✏️ How to create your account

1
Go to the Web Chat site Click the button below — or go to chat.davevoyles.synology.me in any browser on your phone or computer.
2
Click "Sign Up" You'll see a login screen. Click Sign Up (or Create Account) and enter your name, email address, and a password you choose.
3
Start chatting! That's it. You're in. Click the ✏️ pencil icon (or "New Chat") to start a conversation — just like starting a new chat in Gemini.
💡 Tip: Use the same email you use for everything else. There's no spam — it's just for logging in.

🖥️ How to use it

Once you're logged in, it works just like Gemini:

💬
Type your question in the box at the bottom Press Enter or click the send button. The AI replies instantly.
📂
It remembers your conversation Your past chats are saved on the left sidebar. Click any one to go back to it.
✨
Start a new chat Click the ✏️ pencil icon (top left) to start a fresh conversation — like opening a new tab in Gemini.
📁
Share files (optional) Click the 📎 paperclip to attach a document, photo, or file and ask the AI to help with it.
✅ Works on anything: iPhone, Android, Mac, Windows, tablet — any modern web browser. No app to install.

🤷 Slack or Web Chat — which should I use?

Either works! Here's a simple guide:

Use Slack when…

  • You want to use special commands like /ask, /email, or /dropbox
  • You're already in Slack for other things
  • You want to share a file directly from your phone

Use Web Chat when…

  • You want a clean, focused chat experience (like Gemini)
  • You want to keep a long conversation and come back to it later
  • You're on a computer and want a bigger screen
  • You don't feel like opening Slack
