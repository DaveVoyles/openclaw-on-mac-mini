🎯 Basic Commands

Start with these essential commands:

/ask question:What is the weather like today?

Ask me anything! I can answer questions, help with research, or chat about any topic.

/help

See all available commands and what they do.

/analyze-file [attach file]

Upload a PDF, image, or document for AI-powered analysis.

💡 Pro Tip: I support follow-up questions! Just continue the conversation naturally.

⏰ Scheduled Tasks

Automate recurring tasks with the scheduler:

/schedule-task name:Daily Weather cron:0 8 * * *

Create tasks that run automatically on a schedule (using cron syntax).

/list-tasks

View all your scheduled tasks and their status.

/set-reminder time:tomorrow at 2pm message:Call dentist

Set one-time reminders for important events.

💡 Pro Tip: Use scheduled tasks for daily digests, weekly reports, or any recurring automation!
